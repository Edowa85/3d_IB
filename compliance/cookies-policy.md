## What Are Cookies?

**Cookies** are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help provide functionality, analyze usage, and enhance your experience.

### Types of Cookies

| Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Session Cookies** | Temporary storage for navigation | Deleted when browser closes |
| **Persistent Cookies** | Remember preferences across sessions | Expiry date set (typically months/years) |
| **First-Party Cookies** | Set by the website you're visiting | Variable |
| **Third-Party Cookies** | Set by external services | Variable |

## How to Manage Cookies

### Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

| Browser | Cookie Management |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Chrome** | Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data |
| **Firefox** | Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data |
| **Safari** | Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data |
| **Edge** | Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage cookies |

### What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

- **Essential cookies** cannot be disabled - the service will not function
- **Functional cookies** disabled - you may lose personalized features
- **Analytics cookies** disabled - we cannot analyze usage to improve the service
