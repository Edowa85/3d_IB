## Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR, users have the following rights:

### Right to Information (Articles 13 & 14)

Users are informed of:
- What data is collected and why
- How data is used and stored
- Their rights regarding their data
- How to exercise those rights

### Right to Access (Article 15)

Users can request:
- Confirmation of whether their data is being processed
- Access to their personal data
- Information about processing activities

**Request Method:** Contact [CONTACT_EMAIL] with subject "Data Access Request"

### Right to Rectification (Article 16)

Users can request:
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Completion of incomplete data

**Request Method:** Via account settings or [CONTACT_EMAIL]

### Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten) (Article 17)

Users can request deletion of their data when:
- The data is no longer necessary for the original purpose
- The user withdraws consent
- The data has been processed unlawfully

**Request Method:** Account deletion function or [CONTACT_EMAIL]

### Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

Users can request limitation of processing when:
- Accuracy of data is contested
- Processing is unlawful but user opposes erasure
- Data is no longer needed but required for legal claims

### Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Users can receive their data in a structured, machine-readable format and transfer it to another service.

**Request Method:** Contact [CONTACT_EMAIL] with subject "Data Portability Request"

### Right to Object (Article 21)

Users can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

**Request Method:** Contact [CONTACT_EMAIL] with subject "Objection to Processing"

### Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

3Dcard does not use automated decision-making or profiling.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

## Data Breach Procedure

In the event of a personal data breach:

1. **Assessment** - Determine risk to user rights and freedoms
2. **Notification** - Notify supervisory authority within 72 hours if high risk
3. **User Notification** - Inform affected users without undue delay if high risk
4. **Documentation** - Record breach details, impact, and remediation
